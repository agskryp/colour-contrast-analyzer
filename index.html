Colour Contrast Analyzer

Enter lists of text and background hex colours to render every possible pairing. The analyzer calculates each contrast ratio and reports pass/fail results against WCAG Level AA using the selected sample size and weight.

Add one or more hex values in each textarea, separated by new lines, spaces, commas, or semicolons. Adjust the normal and large text sizes, toggle bold when needed, then select Update Results to refresh the samples and summary counts.

WCAG Level AA requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. Large text is 24px or larger at normal weight, or 18.66px or larger when bold.
